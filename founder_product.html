The CycleVault

Product Overview, Founders, Agreement, and Roadmap

1. Product Overview — What The CycleVault Is

The CycleVault is a privacy-first menstrual cycle tracking application designed with a single uncompromising principle: user health data should remain entirely under the user’s control. Unlike conventional health and period tracking applications that rely on cloud storage, user accounts, analytics SDKs, or third-party data pipelines, The CycleVault operates on a strictly local-first architecture.

All personal health data is stored locally on the user’s device and protected by operating-system-level encryption. The application performs no automatic data uploads, silent network calls, or background telemetry. There are no user accounts, no centralized databases, and no data monetization through advertising or resale.

From a user perspective, The CycleVault offers calm, minimal, and reliable cycle tracking: logging periods, symptoms, notes, and visualizing cycles through a calendar-based interface. From a technical perspective, the product prioritizes data integrity, defensive programming, and privacy-preserving design over feature bloat or engagement manipulation.

The CycleVault is intentionally positioned as the ethical opposite of mainstream health apps. Its business model is designed to sustain development without compromising trust, privacy, or user autonomy.

4. Founder Agreement — Role, Authority, and Equity

4.1 Ownership Structure (Conceptual)

The CycleVault’s ownership structure reflects contribution, risk, and responsibility.

  • Moiez Qamar holds a majority ownership and controlling stake due to origination of the product, technical execution, and ongoing product risk.
  • Saif Mairaj holds a significant minority co-founder stake aligned with long-term growth and business contribution.

Exact equity percentages are to be finalized upon formal incorporation and shall be subject to vesting.

4.2 Equity Vesting

All founder equity shall vest over a standard vesting schedule (recommended: four years with a one-year cliff), unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.3 Roles and Authority

Product and Engineering Authority:

The Founder & Chief Product Officer (Moiez Qamar) has final authority over:

  • Product roadmap and design
  • Software architecture and implementation
  • Data storage, encryption, privacy, and security
  • Feature approval or rejection

Business and Growth Authority:

The Head of Growth & Business Operations (Saif Mairaj) has authority over:

  • Marketing and brand strategy
  • External communications
  • Pricing and monetization proposals (subject to product approval)
  • Partnerships and vendor relationships

4.4 Non-Override Clause

No business, marketing, or monetization decision may override or compromise:

  • User privacy guarantees
  • Local-first data storage principles
  • Core product architecture or integrity

4.5 Dispute Resolution

In the event of a disagreement affecting product integrity, privacy, or technical implementation, final decision authority resides with the Founder & Chief Product Officer.

5. Product and Company Roadmap

Version 1 — Core Private Tracker (Free)
  • Period start/end tracking
  • Flow intensity logging
  • Symptom tracking and notes
  • Calendar visualization
  • Cycle history and predictions
  • Local-only storage
  • Data export and full reset
  • No monetization
Version 2 — Privacy & Trust Expansion
  • Full encryption with device-level keys
  • Face ID / Touch ID app lock
  • Privacy dashboard
  • Educational content library
  • Continued zero monetization
Version 3 — Premium & Ethical Monetization
  • Apple In-App Purchase subscriptions
  • Advanced insights and exports
  • Chatbot with strict stateless design
  • Sponsored product listings (non-targeted, labeled)
  • Free tier remains fully functional
Version 4 — Advanced Health Features
  • Optional Apple Health integration (read-only)
  • Lifestyle and trend tracking
  • Irregular cycle pattern detection
  • All computation performed locally
Version 5 — Power User & Transparency Tools
  • Multi-profile support
  • Emergency data wipe
  • Stealth mode
  • Public transparency reports
  • Deep privacy documentation
